Welcome to Odd One Out ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our multiplayer party game.

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to run the game. Your data stays in your own Google account. We don't sell or share your data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Odd One Out, we collect the following information:

  • Display Name: The name you choose to use in the game (stored locally and in game sessions)
  • Session Data: Temporary game session identifiers to maintain your connection
  • Game Activity: Clues submitted, votes cast, and scores earned during gameplay
  • Chat Messages: Messages sent during in-game chat (within your game room only)

2. How We Store Your Data

All game data is stored in Google Sheets within the host's Google account. This means:

  • The game host has full control over the data
  • Data is protected by Google's security measures
  • Data can be deleted at any time by deleting the spreadsheet
  • We (the developers) do not have access to your game data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Enable multiplayer gameplay functionality
  • Maintain game sessions and reconnection capability
  • Display leaderboards and game statistics
  • Facilitate in-game chat between players

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Transfer your data outside of the game environment

Your game data is only visible to:

  • Other players in your current game room (display names, clues, votes)
  • Anyone with access to the Google Sheet where the game is hosted

5. Data Retention

  • Session Data: Automatically cleared when you leave a game room
  • Game History: Stored in Google Sheets until manually deleted
  • Leaderboard Data: Persists until the spreadsheet is deleted
  • Local Storage: Your name preference is saved in your browser until you clear browser data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your game data in the Google Sheet
  • Delete: Remove your data by contacting the game host or clearing your browser data
  • Opt-out: Stop using the game at any time
  • Clear Local Data: Clear your browser's local storage to remove saved preferences

7. Children's Privacy

Odd One Out is a party game suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game only collects display names chosen by users, which should not contain real personal information.

8. Security

Your data security is protected by:

  • Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Google account authentication

9. Third-Party Services

The game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Apps Script: Hosts and runs the game
  • Google Sheets: Stores game data
  • Google Fonts: Provides typography

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
